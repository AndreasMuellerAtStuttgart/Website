Music

This page features music written by me. The sound quality isn't very good because I don't have a band/orchestra behind me to record with real instruments, but I hope it's still enjoyable. "The Final Law" songs are written in Guitar Pro, the orchestra piece is written in MuseScore.

The Final Law

Right now I am working on my first concept album, "The Final Law". It is a dark science fiction tale about a human elite fleeing a destroyed earth and ruling another empire among the stars, while on earth a new civilization arises.

Note: I am currently searching for a singer to add vocals to the material. In the songs written so far the vocal line is replaced by a flute. All songs written and exported to audio with Guitar Pro.
